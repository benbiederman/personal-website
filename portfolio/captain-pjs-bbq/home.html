    About Captain PJ's BBQ

    Ahoy me hearties.

    Captain PJ's and his crew have been touring since 2011. Avast with hard work and constant inspiration, Captain PJ's is taking over the world of BBQ. Buccaneers as well as landlubbers can now enjoy grub with a kick. Aye, it's time for ye to climb on board and impress your friends, family, and co-workers at the next function by inviting Captain PJ's team to help with your casual catering needs.

    Our team is open to all size parties and pride ourselves on the ability to customize to the needs of the customer.
